About Me

👋 Hello! I'm Eman Gouda, a lecturer in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Alexandria University. My passion lies in advancing the frontiers of computer vision, with a particular focus on multi-modal adaptation.

🌟 Beyond my research, I’m passionate about inspiring others through teaching. I have taught programming, artificial intelligence, and computer vision to undergraduate and graduate students, helping them build strong foundations in these fields..

Research Interests

  • Computer Vision - Multi-modal learning and adaptation
  • Machine Learning - Domain adaptation and transfer learning
  • Artificial Intelligence - Cross-modal understanding and generation

Courses I Teach

  • Mobile Development
